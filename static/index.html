// capabilities
Everything you need to
stay safe online
A complete suite of security tools designed for university students and staff who need fast, reliable threat detection.
▭
WHOIS Lookup
Check domain registration history, ownership, and age. Newly registered domains are a major phishing red flag.
⬡
DNS / NSLookup
Resolve A, MX, and NS records. Detects suspicious nameservers and DNS configurations used by malicious hosts.
◈
Port Scanner (Nmap)
Scan for open ports and exposed services. Legitimate sites don't expose database or FTP ports publicly.
◆
SSL Certificate Check
Verify TLS version, issuer, validation level, and expiry to confirm a site's identity is trustworthy.
⊛
Sooty
Checks OpenPhish and PhishTank feeds, plus domain heuristics, to catch known phishing URLs and suspicious domains.
◐
Phishing Catcher
Detects phishing patterns in URLs (lookalike domains, risky hosting, suspicious keywords) and flags high-risk links.
⬢
AI Threat Verdict
AI analyzes the URL instantly and delivers a SAFE / WARNING / DANGER verdict with plain-English explanation.
▦
AI Security Summary
After all tools run, AI synthesizes every finding into one clear jargon-free summary built for non-technical users.
// workflow
How PhishGuard works
Four steps from suspicious link to clear verdict — in seconds.
STEP 01
Paste the URL
Drop any suspicious link you received via email, chat, or social media into the scanner.
STEP 02
Run the Tools
PhishGuard runs WHOIS, DNS, Nmap, and SSL checks simultaneously against the target domain.
STEP 03
AI Analyzes
AI evaluates the URL and all tool results, then generates a risk score and detailed verdict.
STEP 04
Stay Safe
Get a clear SAFE, WARNING, or DANGER verdict — plus actionable tips on what to do next.
